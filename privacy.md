# Syndicate Games Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 3, 2026

At Syndicate Games, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines exactly what data we collect, why we need it, and how you can manage or delete your information.

## 1. What Data We Collect
We only gather minimal information required to run our features and maintain game integrity:
*   **Discord Identifiers:** Your unique Discord User ID and Guild/Server IDs.
*   **Game Progress Data:** Statistics, scores, inventories, and custom economy data associated with your User ID.
*   **Command Content:** Contextual inputs sent directly via bot commands to process your game actions.

## 2. Why We Collect This Data
We process your information strictly for the following purposes:
*   **Core Functionality:** To save your game progress and leaderboard standings across Discord servers.
*   **Account Integrity:** To prevent cheating, botting, or exploitation within Syndicate Games.
*   **Security:** To detect and resolve system errors and technical bugs.
*   *Note: We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.*

## 3. How to Get Your Data Deleted
You retain total control over your information. If you want your data wiped from our database, use one of the following methods:
*   **In-Bot Command:** Run the command `/delete-my-data` in any chat where the bot is present.
*   **Manual Request:** Contact our support team via our official support channel or email us at **[Insert Your Contact Email or Support Server Invite Link]**. 

Once requested, all user profiles, game history, and saved data linked to your Discord ID will be permanently removed from our active databases within 48 hours.
